Product Details+

The Axis Jacket is the centrepiece of the SS'25 drop — engineered for structure, designed for movement. An exaggerated silhouette with dropped shoulders, dual chest pockets, and a clean back seam that gives it a tailored edge without sacrificing comfort.

  • 400GSM heavyweight cotton blend
  • Dropped shoulder construction
  • Dual welt chest pockets
  • Interior zip pocket
  • Brushed metal Entrix hardware
  • Oversized fit — true to our standard sizing
Material & Care+
  • 80% Premium Cotton, 20% Recycled Polyester
  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Do not tumble dry — hang dry recommended
  • Iron on low heat, inside out
  • Do not bleach or dry clean

Fabric sourced from certified sustainable mills. Each jacket uses approximately 1.2kg of raw material with less than 3% waste in our zero-cut production process.

Shipping & Returns+
  • Standard delivery: 5–7 business days
  • Express delivery: 2–3 business days (₹199 extra)
  • Free shipping on all orders above ₹4,999
  • Ships from Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached
Styling Notes+

The Axis Jacket was styled with the Void Cargo Trousers and Core Graphic Tee for the campaign shoot. It works equally well layered over a heavyweight hoodie for winter or thrown over a minimal tee in the transition months.

  • Pairs with: Void Cargo, Techwear Shorts
  • Layer under: nothing — this is the statement
  • Footwear: chunky sneakers or clean white runners
