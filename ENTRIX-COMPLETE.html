Product Details+

The Axis Jacket is the centrepiece of the SS'25 drop — engineered for structure, designed for movement. Exaggerated silhouette with dropped shoulders and dual chest pockets.

  • 400GSM heavyweight cotton blend
  • Dropped shoulder construction
